James and Anne Duderstadt Center
Completion Date Site Address Data Type Scan Type
Fall 2021 2281 Bonisteel Blvd, Ann Arbor, MI 48109 Photogrammetry, LiDAR Site

In this prototype, EIPC brought the research to a graduate architecture studio course to get a first test on how LiDAR scanning and Unreal Engine can support the needs of a graduate-level designer. Team members first held one-day workshops introducing LiDAR scanning and Unreal to the graduate students. The students then participated in scanning the Duderstadt Center, a site that includes a library and many other technological and creative resources on campus. These scans were then integrated into studio projects that ranged in scope from developing a new plug-in in Unreal that examined particle effects in design processes to telling the story of the interactions contained within an Invisible Library.

This prototype marks the first formal introduction of our education research. The collaboration yielded promising hopes for the development of a Lab with workflows and tutorials, student consultants, and technology check-out services that could continue to support future integration of this education research.
