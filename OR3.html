Unknown Unknown Photogrammetry, LiDAR Interior

University of Michigan Health System, Cardiovascular Care Center, Operating Room 3 was scanned using the FARO Focus Premium Scanner and the Freestyle 2 hand scanner. Combining these scans with photogrammetry will allow us to create a more complete point cloud model. This project was possible thanks to EIPC co-lead Professor Robert Adams and Dr. Andrew Ibrahim, both of Taubman College of Architecture and Urban Planning.
