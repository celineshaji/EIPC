Empathy in Point Clouds
PROJECTS CORE PRESS ABOUT CONTACT

Empathy in Point-Clouds [EIPC] is a faculty-student research project led by Dawn Gilpin seeking to redefine the terms for creating radically accessible and inclusive architecture. From buildings and environments to digital infrastructure and urban technologies, the EIPC team utilizes the 3D gaming platform, Unreal Engine, to develop project-based work-flows, point clouds using LiDAR scanners, live performance motion-capture animation, and immersive technologies that locate empathy within the design process. We use the UM campus to prototype, test, and validate complex design methodologies to navigate equally complex and imaginative worlding processes.

With every member having a vital role in shaping the direction of EIPC, we are one research team that continues to innovate the use of immersive tools in architectural education and beyond.
